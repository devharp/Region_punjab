About Restaurant

Check our story

At Region Punjab, our sole passion is to present the essence of Punjab through its delectable cuisine. Located in Pune our restaurant stands as a tribute to the culinary heritage of Punjab. With each dish prepared by skilled chefs using age-old recipes and authentic ingredients, we strive to bring you an unparalleled taste of Punjab's rich gastronomy.
